About Us

Fly Aviation Academy

Fly Aviation Academy was established in 2016 as an NGO and registered as an education trust in the Government of India in 2024. Some skilled and highly trained instructors and other Knowledgeable professors bring out the best in all the students. Fly Aviation Academy aims at bringing the world to you by providing courses that are globally relevant. It provides Industry oriented, professional training and skill development in the emerging sectors like Aviation, Hospitality and Travel.
Parent Institution- SKY Futures Shiksha Samiti
